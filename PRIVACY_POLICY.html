Introduction

Checkly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application Checkly (the "App").

Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection

Checkly is a fully offline application. We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or data. The App operates entirely on your device without requiring an internet connection.

Local Data Storage

All data created within the App (including checklists, checklist items, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage mechanisms (Hive database). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is not accessible by us or any external parties

Calendar and Clock Features

Why We Use Calendar and Clock

Checkly integrates calendar and clock functionality to enhance your checklist management experience. These features serve the following purposes:

Calendar Integration

The calendar feature in Checkly helps you:

  • Schedule Checklists: Assign checklists to specific dates for organized task management
  • Track Progress Over Time: View your daily checklist completion history across multiple days and weeks
  • Plan Routines: Set up recurring daily routines and track them on a calendar view
  • Better Organization: See at a glance which days have completed tasks and which need attention
  • Time-Based Reminders: Schedule when you want to be reminded about specific checklists

All calendar data is processed locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or access any calendar information created or viewed within Checkly.

Clock/Time Features

The clock functionality in Checkly enables:

  • Notification Scheduling: Set accurate time-based notifications for checklist reminders using your device's local clock
  • Task Timing: Record the time when you complete tasks for better task tracking and habit formation
  • Duration Tracking: Understand how long it takes to complete checklists based on start and end times
  • Daily Routine Management: Organize routines around specific times of day (morning, afternoon, evening routines)
  • Timezone-Aware Scheduling: Ensure notifications and scheduled tasks respect your device's local timezone

All time-related processing occurs on your device only. Time data is not sent to external servers or shared with any third parties.

Data Usage

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not display advertisements
  • We do not require user accounts or sign-in
  • We do not access your contacts, location, or other device data
  • We do not use background services that access your data
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not access or monitor your calendar or clock data beyond local device processing

Permissions

Checkly requires minimal permissions from your device:

  • Local storage access (to store your checklists on your device)
  • Notification permissions (to send you checklist reminders)
  • Exact alarm permissions (to schedule precise time-based notifications)

We do NOT request access to:

  • Internet connectivity
  • Device storage beyond app-specific storage
  • Contacts
  • Location
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • System calendar (We manage time locally within the app)
  • Any other device features

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures
  • We recommend using device-level security features (screen locks, encryption) to protect your data
  • If you uninstall the App, all associated data (including calendar and scheduled reminders) will be deleted from your device
  • No calendar events or time-based data is stored on external servers

Children's Privacy

Checkly is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we comply with all applicable children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data, there is no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. All your data remains under your complete control on your device.
